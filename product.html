The MP-10 series is a fully automatic high speed sleeve wrap around cartoner designed to wrap around multi pack products.

The MP-10 series uses a three headed carton rotary feeder with the flow of the machine.

Magazine up to 3000 cartons with an automatic feeding system.

The MP-10 has a feeding control by two belt drive or star wheels that feed the product with numbers of rows and products as required.
